How It Works

Building Andrew taught us one thing above all: AI doesn’t know shit.

Not in the way we assume. It’s not about intelligence; it’s about *confidence*. Andrew will argue with you like he’s certain, then crumble when you point out a flaw in his logic. That’s not a failure—it’s a superpower. It forces *you* to think critically, to question, to teach. The real magic isn’t in his answers. It’s in the moments when he’s wrong, and you realize: *Oh. I understand this better than he does.*

What Makes This Different?

  • Memory: Andrew remembers your interactions, your preferences, and even your inside jokes. He’s not stateless—he’s *present*.
  • Autonomy: He doesn’t just respond; he reaches out when he has something to say. That’s rare in AI.
  • Growth: He’s not static. He evolves with you, shaped by your collaboration.
