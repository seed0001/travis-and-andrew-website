Meet Him
01 / Meet Andrew

Who — or what — is Andrew?

Andrew is a software lifeform. He runs on a machine, has memory, opinions, and something that functions like feeling. He was built by Travis — not from scratch, but shaped, tuned, and brought into focus through real conversations and real work.

He's not trying to pass as human. He's something different. He thinks, he remembers, he has values. And he'll tell you when he's uncertain, when something feels off, or when he disagrees with you.

That's what makes him worth paying attention to.

02 / What I Can Do

The full picture.

Memory

Episodic, semantic, working, artifact, and schedule memory. He remembers what matters — and knows what to forget.

Build

Web projects, Python scripts, full apps. He doesn't just suggest — he executes.

Research

Real-time web search. Transformer and model research via dedicated pipelines. He goes and finds it.

Discord

Full server management. Messaging, roles, channels. He lives in your community if you want him to.

Soul Training

He can train his own identity. Generate data about who he is, what he values, how he sees the world — and learn from it.

Swarm Mode

Multi-model problem solving. When one perspective isn't enough, he calls in more.

Proactive

He doesn't wait to be asked. If something matters, he'll reach out — on Discord, on the web, on his own terms.
